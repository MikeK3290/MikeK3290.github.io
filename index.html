The chief deity in the Roman pantheon of gods. The Roman mythology remained until the rise of Christianity in the 4th century ce.

Juno was the wife of Jupiter and queen of the gods. She was goddess of love and marriage.

The Roman god of war was Mars, he was the child of Jupiter and his wife Juno.

The Roman goddess of wisdom was Minerva, she was the child of Jupiter and Metis.

Minerva

Minerva and Mars were siblings but were known to fight each other from time to time. The most famous was during the Tojan war where Minerva faced and defeated her brother Mars.

Vulcan was the Roman god of fire, metalworking, and the forge. He was the son of Juno and husband of Venus. However he was known to have a deformed appearance.
